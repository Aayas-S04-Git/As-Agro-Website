Our Story

AS Agro is a leading provider of agricultural solutions in Maharashtra, committed to empowering farmers with quality products and expert support. Our journey began in 2010 when we started as a small family business, selling seeds and fertilizers to local farmers. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted brand, known for our commitment to quality and customer service.

Today, we offer a wide range of products, including seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and farm equipment, to help farmers improve their crop yield and profitability. We also provide expert advice and support to help farmers make informed decisions and achieve success in their farming operations.

At AS Agro, we are passionate about agriculture and are dedicated to promoting sustainable and environmentally friendly farming practices. We believe that by working together with farmers, we can help build a brighter future for agriculture in India.

Thank you for choosing AS Agro as your partner in agriculture. We look forward to serving you and helping you achieve your farming goals.
